Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Thesis Editor ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Google Docs add-on.

Information We Collect

Thesis Editor accesses only the content of the Google Document you are currently working on. We collect:

  • Document Content: We read your document content solely to provide formatting, word count, and section navigation features. This data is processed locally and is not transmitted to external servers.
  • Document Properties: We store your references and preferences in the document's properties, which remain within your Google account.

How We Use Your Information

We use the accessed information exclusively to:

  • Display section navigation and word counts
  • Apply academic formatting to your document
  • Store and retrieve your reference list
  • Export your document as PDF

Data Storage

Thesis Editor does not store any of your data on external servers. All data remains within your Google account and the document itself. Reference information is stored in the document's properties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your document content never leaves Google's servers through our add-on.

Google API Services

Thesis Editor's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Since all data processing occurs within Google's infrastructure, your data benefits from Google's security measures.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@thesiseditor.app
